Yes. All programs offered by GLA Online are duly recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC). Degrees awarded through the online mode are valid and hold equivalence to regular on-campus degrees, in accordance with UGC regulations.

GLA Online offers the following programs:

  • Master of Business Administration (MBA)
  • Master of Computer Applications (MCA)
  • Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)
  • Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)
  • Bachelor of Commerce (Hons.)

No. As per UGC guidelines, a degree obtained through an approved online program from a recognized university is considered equivalent to a regular on-campus degree in all academic and professional aspects.

All programs are delivered entirely through a Digital Learning Management System (LMS). The LMS provides access to pre-recorded video lectures, live interactive sessions, e-books, quizzes, formative assessments, and discussion forums for collaborative learning.

  1. Visit the official website Go to glaonline.com and begin the application process by selecting “Apply Now.” Choose the Online Campus option to start your registration.
  2. Log in to your account Use the login credentials created during the initial registration to access the application portal.
  3. Complete the registration form Enter all required information, including personal details, parent/guardian details, and residential information.
  4. Pay the registration fee Submit the non-refundable registration fee of ₹2,000 to proceed further.
  5. Provide educational details Fill in your academic information and generate your DEB ID as instructed.
  6. Upload necessary documents Submit all required documents along with the mandatory undertaking.
  7. Pay the admission fee Complete the process by paying the applicable admission fee.

Upon successful completion of the program, the degree certificate is issued by GLA Online University through the following modes:
