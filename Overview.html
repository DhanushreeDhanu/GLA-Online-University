Everything began with a belief - education should never be a privilege, but a possibility for every child.

A belief held by the Late Shri Ganeshi Lal Agrawal - that every child deserves an education, irrespective of their circumstances. When he passed away, his son, Shri Narayan Das Agrawal, still in his teens, took over the weight of family responsibilities. His own education paused, but his father’s dream continued to guide him.

Holding on to that purpose, he laid the foundation of GLA Online University - in Mathura - built not just with resources, but with resilience, empathy, and a commitment to make learning accessible to all.

What began decades ago has now shaped the futures of 46,000+ GLAdiators, proving that when education becomes reachable, dreams truly take flight!

#ApneSapneApneShaharSe

Our goal is to make quality education accessible to learners at every nook and corner of the globe.

We offer flexible and affordable online programs tailored to the needs of modern learners. Focusing on emerging fields, we aim to build skills, foster innovation, and nurture future leaders and entrepreneurs. Our commitment to academic excellence and learner success remains at the heart of everything we do.

Vision

To make higher education globally accessible and affordable for every learner through industry-aligned, job-oriented online learning that enhances employability and prepares future-ready professionals.

Mission

  • To provide high-quality online education at an affordable cost.
  • To make online education accessible beyond geographical, economic, and temporal boundaries.
  • To offer flexible learning pathways with learner-centric schedules and adaptable academic structures.
  • To enhance employability through industry-aligned, job-oriented curricula that build future-ready skills.
